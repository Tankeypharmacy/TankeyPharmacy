About us

Neighbourhood care, done properly.

Tankey Pharmacy started with one idea: that getting your medicine shouldn't feel like a chore. We stock the essentials every household needs, keep clear records of your prescriptions so refills are quick, and take the time to explain dosage in plain language — not just hand over a bag and a receipt.

Every product on our shelves is sourced through licensed distributors and checked against NAFDAC registration before it reaches you.
